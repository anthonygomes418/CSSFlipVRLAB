Startup Accelerator & Incubator

The Startup Accelerator helps innovators transition from science and other projects to startups, and help the startups build businesses by connecting them with customers, capital, experts, and a community of entrepreneurs and influencers. The Startup Accelerator offers a diversity of services, such as mentorship, IP protection, and an environment for entrepreneurs to launch, fund, scale, and grow their businesses.

It offers educational programming to support student, faculty, and community innovators and entrepreneurs.

  • JSU I-Corps Site
  • Southeast Xlerator Network
  • Lean Startup Training
  • Innovation Bootcamps
  • Speaker Series
  • Hackathons
  • Competitions
  • 1:1 Coaching
  • Virtual Mentoring
  • Copyrighting, Trademarking, and Patenting
  • SBIR/STTR Capabilities Matching/Grant Writing
  • Co-Working Space
