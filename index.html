The Center for Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Economic Development (CIEED)


The Center for Innovation, Entrepreneurship and Economic Development (CIEED) will leverage the best of JSU’s STEM, business, and entrepreneurial capabilities, as well as collaborative potential in addressing some of society’s most pressing programs (via university-industry partnerships). The CIEED will take advantage of expertise co-location and facilitate the intersection of widely disparate learning and idea generation; a place for constant learning, common vision, as well as creativity and innovation. Researchers from across various disciplines will be able to design, develop, and innovate together from multidisciplinary perspectives and approaches. The research efforts will include design thinking and strategizing.
