VR/AR Lab

The VR/AR Academy is a training and testing lab for the latest in virtual reality and augmented reality technology. The Academy provides training for those interested in creating their own VR/AR application or just need to experiment with the latest equipment.

The goal is for JSU faculty to develop VR/AR applications to enhance the teaching and learning environment.Researchers from across various disciplines will be able to design, develop, and innovate together from multidisciplinary perspectives and approaches. The research efforts will include design thinking and strategizing.
