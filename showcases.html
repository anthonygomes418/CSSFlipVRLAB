Showcases and Competitions

CIIED will organize and host competitions where students can showcase their ideas. Winners will be sponsored to participate in national and international competitions as well as receiving prizes.

The teams will also be provided support to develop and ameliorate their projects, for example, help with ensuring their ownership and intellectual property.
