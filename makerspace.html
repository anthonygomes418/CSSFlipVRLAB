Makerspace - TigerLab

The TigerLab will focus on creativity, collaboration and building a community where students can learn, experiment with new tools, develop skills, and become innovators and designers. Equipped with co-working spaces, 3-D printers and other equipment, web conferencing capabilities, and other resources, the makerspace will be available to students for coalescing and ideation.

Available in the TigerLab are:

  • 3D Printers
  • Printers
  • Embroidery Machine
  • Laser Cutter
  • Sewing Machine
  • Iron and Ironing Board
  • Software Resources
