CIIED Mentorship


The CIIED is seeking successful alumni entrepreneurs to help build the entrepreneurial culture at Jackson State University with personal mentoring, network introductions, and engagement. The CIEED will take advantage of expertise co-location and facilitate the intersection of widely disparate learning and idea generation; a place for constant learning, common vision, as well as creativity and innovation.
